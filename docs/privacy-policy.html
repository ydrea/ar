Privacy Policy for Islands Atlas

Effective date: 17 July 2026
Last updated: 17 July 2026

Islands Atlas, also referred to as AR Atlas Otoka, is developed and published by ydrea. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how that information is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information accessed by the app

Camera

Islands Atlas uses the device camera to display a live augmented-reality view. Camera images are processed as a live preview on the user's device. The app does not record, store, upload, or share camera images or video.

Location

Islands Atlas requests access to the device's precise foreground location. Location is used to:

  • determine the user's position;
  • calculate direction and distance to geographic points of interest;
  • place geographic labels and markers in the augmented-reality view.

Location information is processed on the user's device. ydrea does not store, upload, sell, or share the user's location. The app does not request or use background location.

Motion and orientation sensors

The app accesses device motion, compass, and orientation sensor information to determine which direction the device is pointing and to align augmented-reality content with the surrounding environment. This sensor information is processed on the user's device. ydrea does not store, upload, or share this information.

App update information

The app may communicate with the Expo update service to check for and download application updates. When such a request is made, the update provider may process standard technical information required to deliver the update, such as the app version, platform, update channel, network address, and request metadata. This information is used only to provide and maintain application updates.

2. Personal information

Islands Atlas does not require users to create an account or sign in. The app does not intentionally collect:

  • names;
  • email addresses;
  • phone numbers;
  • contact lists;
  • photographs or videos;
  • payment information;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • user-generated content.

3. Advertising and analytics

Islands Atlas does not display third-party advertisements. This version of the app does not use third-party advertising, behavioural tracking, or analytics services.

4. Sharing of information

ydrea does not sell users' personal information. Camera, location, and orientation information used by the augmented-reality features is not shared with third parties. Limited technical request information may be processed by the application update provider solely to check for and deliver software updates.

5. Data storage and retention

Islands Atlas does not maintain user accounts or a remote database containing users' camera, location, or sensor information. Temporary application state and preferences may be stored locally on the user's device. Users can remove locally stored app information by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Because ydrea does not retain user accounts, camera images, location history, or sensor history on its servers, there is normally no remotely stored personal information to delete.

6. Permissions

The app requests only permissions required for its augmented-reality functionality. Users may deny or revoke camera or location permissions through their device settings. Some augmented-reality features will not function without those permissions.

7. Data security

The app is designed to minimise data collection by processing camera, location, and orientation information locally on the user's device. Reasonable technical measures are used to protect the app and its update communications. However, no electronic system or network transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's privacy

Islands Atlas is not specifically designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app's functionality or data practices change. The updated version will be published at this same address together with a revised “Last updated” date.
